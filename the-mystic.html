The Mystic

From darkness to light.

My journey began in the shadows of anxiety, depression, and disease—a period of my life defined by confusion and disconnection. I felt lost, burdened by the weight of expectations and emotions I didn’t understand. But through that darkness, I found the light that would guide me forward: the healing power of spirituality, intuition, and cartomancy.

Tarot and oracle decks became my companions in healing. Through them, I reconnected with my inner self, slowly rediscovering the joy that life offers when we embrace our own truth. Today, I live in alignment with what feels light, joyful, and energetically aligned with my soul’s purpose. I’m here to share the tools, practices, and lessons that helped me transform my life—so you can do the same. Whether you're searching for clarity, healing, or simply deeper insight, this space is for you.
